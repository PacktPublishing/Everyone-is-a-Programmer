#### **1. Complex gamification and incentive system**

_The core motivation of our MVP is the simple yet powerful “Streak”. _

- **DO NOT DO**: Customizable avatar, equipment, skill tree.
- **DO NOT DO**: Narrative driven quest or journey.
- **What Not to Do**: Variable, random reward system.

#### **2. All social and community features**

_MVP stage, the user's relationship is established with "self" rather than with other users. _

- **Don'ts**: Accountability Partners, Team Challenges, Guilds.
- **DO NOT DO**: Leaderboard.
- **DO NOT DO**: Social updates or the ability to share milestones.

#### **3. Comprehensive health and diary functions**

_Our MVP is a “habit tracker”, not a “universal health manager”. _

- **What not to do**: Guided journaling or mood tracking.
- **DO NOT DO**: Integrated meditation or breathing exercises.
- **What not to do**: Coping strategies based on scientific theory (CBT/DBT).

#### **4. Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Hyper-Personalization**

_We won’t invest resources in building complex AI features until we have enough data and user feedback. _

- **What not to do**: Conversational AI coaching.
- **What not to do**: Predictive analytics and dynamic target adjustment.
- **What Not to Do**: Natural Language Processing (NLP)-based diary analysis.

#### **5. Automation and third-party platform integration**

_At its core, MVP is “conscious” manual tracking to help users build awareness of their own behavior. _

- **DO NOT DO**: Automatic synchronization with health platforms (Apple Health/Google Fit).
- **DO NOT DO**: Smart reminders based on context (location/calendar).
- **DON'T**: API integration with IFTTT/Zapier.
