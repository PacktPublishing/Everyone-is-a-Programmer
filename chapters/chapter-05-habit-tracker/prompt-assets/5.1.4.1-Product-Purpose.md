#### **1. Problem Statement**

In today's fast-paced modern life, many motivated individuals find themselves trapped in a common dilemma: they desire self-improvement, but are overwhelmed by daily stress and chaos. Whether they are students struggling under academic pressure like **Li Jing**, or professionals seeking a balance between work and life like **Wang Chen**, their common pain point is that the process of establishing and maintaining positive habits often becomes another new source of stress**.

Existing tools are either too fragmented to provide sustained momentum or too complex and require a lot of effort to manage. This leads to a common negative cycle: users start with enthusiasm, but quickly become frustrated when their plans are disrupted or miss a day or two, and eventually give up entirely.

The core problem is: **Users lack a tool that is simple, smart, and motivating enough to help them easily gain a sense of control and continuous motivation for progress in their chaotic lives. **

#### **2. Target users**

Our Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will prioritize serving "ambitious but overwhelmed" growth users**.

Specifically, our core target users are:

- **Stressed students (such as Li Jing)**: They need structured guidance and positive emotional support to establish basic learning and healthy habits to cope with anxiety.
- **Busy young professionals (such as Wang Chen)**: They pursue efficiency and work-life balance, and need a seamless, low-friction tool that can easily integrate personal growth goals into their tight schedules.

For data-driven advanced users like **Zhang Wei**, their needs for deep data analysis and multi-platform integration, although extremely valuable, will be the focus of our future version iterations, rather than the core of the MVP stage.

#### **3. Core Values**

Our Habit Tracker MVP is designed to directly address these issues by providing an experience that is “easy, positive, and in control.” Our core value proposition is: **Let you see your progress effortlessly and fall in love with the process of self-improvement. **

We will achieve this value through the following three points:

1. **Provide a clear sense of structure**: Gather all users’ thoughts about personal growth into a unified and refreshing dashboard, instantly reducing the user’s psychological burden.
2. **Create a frictionless tracking experience**: With extremely simple "one-click completion" operations and flexible scheduling options, the act of "recording habits" itself becomes an easy and satisfying "small victory".
3. **Build sustainable intrinsic motivation**: The core incentive mechanism of MVP will focus on the visualization of "Continuous Record (Streak)". By clearly demonstrating the user's continuous efforts, we help them build valuable **"senses of competence"** and **"sense of control"**.
