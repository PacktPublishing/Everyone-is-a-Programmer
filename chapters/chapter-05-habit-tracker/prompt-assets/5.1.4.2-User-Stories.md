#### **Topic 1: Habit Creation and Recording**

- **User Story 1.1 (Wang Chen)**: “As a **busy professional**, I would like to **quickly create and flexibly schedule a habit** (e.g. ‘work out 3 times a week’) so that I can **make my personal growth plan fit seamlessly into my changing work schedule**.”
- **User Story 1.2 (Wang Chen)**: "As an **efficiency-focused manager**, I hope to be able to **complete the check-in** with one click, so that **recording habits itself will not become another to-do task for me**."
- **User Story 1.3 (Li Jing)**: "As a student** who wants to establish a sense of order, I hope to **concrete the goal in my mind into a habit with an icon**, so that I can **convert the vague anxiety into a clear, executable plan**."
- **User Story 1.4 (Li Jing)**: "As a student who **feels easily** easily, I would like to be able to **track the habits I want to develop and the habits I want to quit** at the same time, so that I can **gain a sense of control over my life** from both the positive and negative aspects."

#### **Topic 2: Daily Reminder**

- **User Story 2.1 (Wang Chen)**: "As a manager whose **schedule is full of work**, I hope to **set an effective reminder** for my personal habits, so that it can **stand out among the many work notifications and pull me back to the track of my personal life**."

#### **Topic 3: Progress Tracking and Statistics**

- **User Story 3.1 (Wang Chen)**: "As a professional who values ​​return on investment, I would like to be able to quickly browse my habit completion history chart** so that I can judge at a glance whether the time I invest in personal growth is worth it**."
