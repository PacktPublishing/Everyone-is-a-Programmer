- **2023annual market size**: Approximately $1.5 billion

#### Main user groups

**Creative Workers (30%)**

- **ability to pay**: Medium to high (monthly budget 200-1,000 yuan)

**Content Creators (40%)**
- **crowd**: Self-media blogger, video producer, writer
- **need**: Picture generation, cover production, material creation
- **ability to pay**: Medium (monthly budget 100-500 yuan)
- **Frequency of use**: Medium to High (5-20 spawns per week)

**Marketers (25%)**
- **crowd**: Advertising planning, brand promotion, e-commerce operations
- **need**: Marketing materials, product display, advertising creativity
- **ability to pay**: High (monthly budget 500-2000 yuan)
- **Frequency of use**: Medium (3-15 spawns per week)

**Individual users (5%)**
- **crowd**: Ordinary consumers, students, enthusiasts
- **need**: Entertainment, learning, personal projects
- **ability to pay**: Low (monthly budget 50-200 yuan)
- **Frequency of use**: Low (1-5 spawns per week)

### 3.3 Pricing Psychology

- The highest price package serves as an anchor to highlight the cost-effectiveness of the mid-price package

- Show other users' preferences
- Showcase user reviews and use cases
- Provide recommendation reward mechanism

## 6. Risk assessment and response

### 6.1 market risk

#### competition intensifies
- **risk**: Major manufacturers enter the market and start a price war
- **response**: Differentiated positioning, technological innovation, user stickiness

#### market saturation
- **risk**: User growth slows
- **response**: Market segmentation, internationalization, new feature development

### 6.2 technology risk

#### API Depend on
- **risk**: Replicate API Unstable or rising prices
- **response**: many API Support, self-built model ability

#### Technical updates
- **risk**: Rapid iteration of new technologies
- **response**: Continuous investment in technology and rapid adaptation

### 6.3 business risk

#### cash flow
- **risk**: Large initial investment, long payback period
- **response**: Financing planning, cost control

#### User churn
- **risk**: Low user retention rate
- **response**: Product optimization, user operations

### 6.4 Compliance risk

#### Content moderation
- **risk**: Generate inappropriate content
- **response**: Strict content review and user education

#### Copyright issues
- **risk**: Generate content copyright disputes
- **response**: Legal terms, insurance protection
